Goals

  • Education: We aim to educate the younger generation by allowing them to simulate living in a slum
  • Empathy: By letting the younger generation step into the shoes of a poor person, the game gives a real life opportunity to think and act as a person living in a slum would, something that cannot be achieved by just looking at pictures or reading statistics. Hence, we hope to reach out to their hearts.
  • Empowerment: Through education and empathy, we hope to inspire people to take action
